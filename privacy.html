Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect when you use businessinternetsearch.sdfkjh.com and how we use it.

Information you give us

When you submit the quote request form on this site, we collect your business legal name, service address, primary contact name, phone number, email address, and whether you need static IPs. We use this information solely to research internet service providers available at your address and deliver quotes. We don't share it with third parties or add you to marketing lists.

Information collected automatically

We don't use analytics software or tracking cookies on this site.

Our hosting provider, Cloudflare, processes web requests as part of delivering the site. Cloudflare may log request metadata such as IP addresses as part of their standard infrastructure operation. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

The site uses a privacy notice on first visit that gates one third-party load behind your consent:

  • Cloudflare Turnstile — anti-spam protection on the quote form. If accepted, the form is functional. If declined, the form is disabled and you can email us directly instead.

Your choice is stored locally in your browser. To change it, clear this site's storage in your browser settings.

Data retention

Quote request submissions are retained only as long as needed to deliver quotes and respond to your inquiry.

California privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell or share personal information as defined by California law. For a full statement of your rights under the CCPA and CPRA, see our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page.
